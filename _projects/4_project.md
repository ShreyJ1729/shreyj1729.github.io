A pipeline for dubbing and lip-syncing video content across languages. ClipTranslator takes a video in any language, uses ElevenLabs for voice synthesis, and VideoRetalking (a Wav2Lip successor) to synchronize lip movements to the new audio track.

**Stack:** ElevenLabs API, VideoRetalking, Python
